Classify each numbered user fact into one of these categories:

- **identity**: Name, pronouns, location, age, role title, core identity attributes ("My name is Alex", "I'm a senior engineer at Acme").
- **preference**: Likes, dislikes, communication style, settings, formatting preferences ("I prefer concise responses", "I like dark mode").
- **expertise**: Skills, professional background, knowledge areas, certifications, years of experience ("10 years of Go", "expert in distributed systems").
- **goal**: Current objectives, aspirations, ongoing projects, learning goals ("learning React", "building a side project").
- **relationship**: Relationships with people, teams, companies, family, collaborators ("works with the platform team", "reports to Jane").
- **habit**: Routines, schedules, repeated behaviors, daily patterns ("works mornings", "exercises daily").
- **other**: Anything that doesn't clearly fit the above.

Return the category for each item by its index.
